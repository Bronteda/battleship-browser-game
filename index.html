🧭 How to Play Battleships

🎯 Objective:

Place your ships and sink the computer’s hidden fleet before yours are sunk!

🛠️ Game Setup:

  • You have one 10x10 grid.
  • You’ll place 4 ships of different sizes:
    • Ship 5 – occupies 5 cells
    • Ship 4 – occupies 4 cells
    • Ship 3 – occupies 3 cells
    • Ship 2 – occupies 2 cells

🛳️ Placing Your Ships:

  1. Click a ship button (e.g., Ship 5) on the right panel to select it.
  2. Choose the orientation – Horizontal or Vertical.
  3. Click on the grid to place the ship. It will highlight in a different color.
  4. Repeat for all ships. Once all are placed, the "Let's Play" button will appear.

🎮 Gameplay:

  • Once the game starts, you’ll take turns with the computer.
  • Click on a grid square to fire at that location.
  • Hit: The square is marked in red.🔴
  • Miss: The square is marked in yellow.🟡
  • If all cells of a ship are hit, that ship is sunk and those squares turn grey.

🔁 Game Controls:

  • Reposition Your Ships: Clear your ship placements and start positioning again.
  • Play Again: Restarts the game after a win or loss.
  • Reset 🔄️: Returns you to the very beginning of the setup phase.

💡 Tips:

  • Place ships with space between them to make them harder to find.
  • Track your guesses and hits to deduce where the next ship might be.
  • Think strategically—don't waste turns on already-guessed locations.
